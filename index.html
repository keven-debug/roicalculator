Tips to improve your procurement operations

1
Centralise order tracking in one platform
Buyers spend up to 40% of their day chasing order status across emails, ERP screens, and supplier portals. A single source of truth cuts this dramatically.
2
Set up proactive late-order alerts
Don't wait for production to flag a missing part. Automated supplier confirmations and deviation alerts let you act weeks earlier.
3
Give non-buyers self-serve visibility
Every time a project manager or ops lead asks a buyer for a status update, it costs 15-30 minutes. Dashboards and AI chatbots eliminate this interrupt.
4
Start measuring supplier on-time performance
You can't improve what you don't measure. A structured supplier scorecard reduces late deliveries by an average of 40% within 6 months.
